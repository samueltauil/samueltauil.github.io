If you spend your day in Visual Studio Code, mastering a few shortcuts pays back every single minute. Here are a handful I reach for constantly:

## Essential Shortcuts

**Command Palette** — access any command fast:
- macOS: `⇧⌘P`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl+Shift+P`

**Quick Open** — jump to files by name:
- macOS: `⌘P`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl+P`

**Multi-cursor magic** — place cursors with `Alt+Click`, add one per selected line:
- macOS: `⇧⌥I`
- Windows/Linux: `Shift+Alt+I`

Select next match:
- macOS: `⌘D`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl+D`

**Toggle Sidebar** — declutter your view:
- macOS: `⌘B`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl+B`

**Zen Mode** — eliminate distractions:
- macOS: `⌘K Z`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl+K Z`

## Customize Your Workflow

Want to tailor shortcuts to your workflow? Open Keyboard Shortcuts (File/Code → Preferences → Keyboard Shortcuts) and make them your own.
